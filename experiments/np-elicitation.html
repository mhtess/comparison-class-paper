Instructions

We are interested in objects or situations described with different adjectives. In particular, we would like you to fill in sentences like the following:

As, Bs, and Cs are all kinds of Xs.
As are generally green.
Bs are generally yellow.
Cs are sometimes green and sometimes yellow.

For example: Cucumbers, Squashes, and Bell Peppers are all kinds of vegetables. Cucumbers are generally green while Squashes are generally yellow. Bell Peppers are sometimes green and sometimes yellow.

Tip: It may be easier to first think up the three kinds of things, and then figure out what the common category (like "seasons") is.

There are a total of 15 trials.
